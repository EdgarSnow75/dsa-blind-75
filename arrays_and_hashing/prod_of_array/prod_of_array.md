I used a nested looping to append the product of all numbers inside the list except for the current iteration of the outer loop. It is O(n^2) due to the nested list but we could get O(2n) by using prefixes and suffixes and looping through the list twice without any nesting in order to fill out the modified list with prefix products first and then suffix products (the products of all the numbers in an array except self is the product of all the numbers that come before which are prefixes and the product of all the numbers that come after which are suffixes.)
